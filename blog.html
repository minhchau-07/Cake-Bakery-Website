Simple Sweet Recipes to Try at Home

Want to make delicious sweets yourself? Here’s a simple butter cookie recipe:

Ingredients:
200g unsalted butter
100g sugar
300g all-purpose flour
1 egg A pinch of salt

Instructions:
1. Cream the butter and sugar until smooth.
2. Add the egg and mix well.
3. Sift in the flour and salt, and knead into a dough.
4. Shape into cookies and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15 minutes.
